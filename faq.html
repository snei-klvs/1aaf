Getting Started

What is PlayStation™Vue?
  • PlayStation™Vue is a new TV service that streams live TV, movies, sports, and more on Android tablets and phones. You must have a PlayStation Network ID and subscription to use the the PlayStation™Vue service.
What’s different about PlayStation™Vue on Android?
  • With PlayStation™Vue on Android, you’ll be able to use the great streaming features available on your PlayStation®4, PlayStation®3 systems, Fire TV, iOS, as well as the same intuitive interface. Enjoy full access to Timeline, My Shows, Favorite Channels, Search, and Recommendations, and stream Live, On Demand, Catchup, or DVR content, just like you do on your console.
  • Explore and Guide functionality are not currently available on Android devices but will be added over time.
  • You must create your profile on a PlayStation system or a Fire TV device.
  • You cannot purchase subscription packages or adjust your account or billing information on Android devices.
  • Some channels are not available through PlayStation™Vue Mobile due to streaming rights, and will be marked as such with a Mobile Restricted badge.

Key Features

My Shows
  • The My Shows feature is your ultimate DVR. Shows you have tagged as My Shows will be automatically saved and stored to the cloud, without conflicts. Every newly aired episode marked "DVR" will be saved in My Shows for up to 28 days. On Demand episodes of these shows will be saved for different durations of time.
Live TV
  • The Live TV section shows your currently airing content in order of popularity. This gives you an easy way to see what shows are trending so you never miss the big game or breaking news.
  • Opening the full screen view of Live TV shows you everything that’s currently airing across all your available networks.
Timeline
  • A personalized Timeline displays what you’re currently watching and what’s up next. You can view the last shows you watched, making it easy to return to the shows you were watching or look ahead and record a show you might want to watch in the future.
Search
  • With Search you can easily find your favorite program, shows, movies, and channels by Title or Keyword. Select the magnifying glass icon at the top of the screen and use the keyboard to enter in whatever you are looking for.

Troubleshooting

Why do ALL shows appear as "Mobile Restricted"?
  • If you experience all channels being marked as Mobile Restricted, you will need to re-establish your home location on your mobile device. To do so, simply select Sign Out from the PS Vue mobile menu, connect your device to the Home Network where you originated your PlayStation™Vue subscription, and log back into the PlayStation™Vue Mobile application.
How much bandwidth do I need?
  • 10 Mbps or higher is recommended for the best experience, plus 5 Mbps for every additional stream on Wifi. LTE or 4G is recommended when using cellular.
I’m having trouble streaming.
  • Please check your network connection on your device.
  • If issues persist, please restart your device and try the application again.
  • Finally, if you continue having problems, please unplug your modem, and router from power for 60 seconds, plug them back in, and restart the PlayStation™Vue application or contact your cellular network provider for assistance.

Errors

I’m receiving an error code.
Error Problem Solution

1001, 1003, 1004, 1005 Content Loading Please try loading the program again or selecting another program. If the problems persist, please check your network connection and try again.
1008, 1009, 1010, 1011, 1014, 1019 Content Loading Please contact customer support. There may be a problem with your account either due to location, subscription, or how many devices you are attempting to access the PlayStation™Vue service from.
