Home Screen

PlayStation™Vue launches with the Home Screen, which includes the Main Menu and Action Bar. If you launch the app in portrait mode it shows a carousel of popular content.

The Main Menu includes:

  • Profile Selection
  • Home
  • My Shows
  • Channels
  • Live TV
  • Recently Watched
  • Featured
  • Settings
  • Sign Out

Settings includes:

  • FAQ
  • PlayStation™Vue User Guide
  • Terms of Service
  • Privacy Policy
  • Nielsen Opt-Out
  • About PlayStation™Vue

How to Use the Main Menu and Action Bar

Simply tap the Menu button on top left of the screen to display the Main Menu options. Tap on the magnifying glass on the top right to open Search.

  • Search – use the visual keyboard on your device to input letters for the name of the program, show, or channel.
  • Profiles - select the profile you wish to view with. You can add multiple sub-profiles each with its own name, avatar, personalized show and channel favorites and watch history while accessing your account from a PlayStation console or Fire TV device. The application will reload the content when you select a different profile.
  • Settings – navigates to the Settings section to view the FAQ, User Guide, and other documents regarding the application.

How to Watch TV

To watch live TV or shows playing now:

  • Simply press on any show in the Live TV strand to start viewing.
  • Video can be watched in either landscape or portrait mode.
  • While video is playing tap the screen to bring up the player controls to play, pause, seek, and get info on the program.
  • Go to My Shows to see what shows you have DVR’d.
  • Go to Channel page to see what shows are available by different networks.
  • Go to Recently Watched programs to finish watching what you previously watched.
  • Go to the Featured page to see what shows are currently being promoted.

To watch a show you missed earlier:

  • Find it from the Main Menu if you added it to My Shows.
  • Search for the show and add to your My Shows or watch the VOD airing, if available.

To find a specific program, show, or channel:

  • Tap the Search icon in the Action Bar. Use the on-screen keyboard to enter letters for the name of the program, show, or channel.

How to Find What to Watch

Don't know what to watch?

  • Scroll down to Live TV to see what is currently playing on Live TV.
  • Scroll down to My Shows to quickly see any episodes have recorded for shows you DVR.
  • Scroll down to Favorite Channels to choose shows from the channels you added.
  • Scroll down to Featured Shows to see what the networks have curated.
  • Scroll down to Recently Watched to resume watching a previously viewed program.

To find a show you watched previously, but which is not now on:

  • Go to Recently Watched and find the show. Some shows may not be available for viewing after the original airing.
  • Go to Search to find shows you previously watched. Some shows may not be available for viewing after the original airing.
